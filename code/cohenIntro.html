Thank you for your interest in our experiment!

In this experiment, you will see a very fast display of letters and numbers appear on the screen. Count each time a number appears (For example, if you see A, B, C, 1, 5, D, you would report having counted "2" numbers). At the end of each trial you will be prompted to tell us how many numbers you counted. Please read the instructions carefully on every trial--they may change throughout the experiment. This experiment is quite difficult, please don't get frustrated! Take each trial seriously and make your best effort to answer all of the questions.

Please note: This experiment runs in full screen mode. If you exit full screen mode during the experiment your data will NOT be saved and the HIT will not be reported as completed! Please turn off background programs that might interfere with your browser BEFORE pressing start and do not exit full screen mode during the experiment. If your browser does not support full screen mode you MUST upgrade. You can check here to see whether your browser supports full screen.

You will be paid $0.30 for participating in this experiment. We expect the experiment to take no more than 5 minutes.
